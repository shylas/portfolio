Why should my site be responsive to different devices?

Mobile

Over 36% of mobile subscribers use iPhones or iPads to read email and 34% of subscribers only use mobile devices to read emails. (Informz)

Phablets and Tablets

Tablet devices account for the highest add-to-cart rates on e-commerce websites at 8.58%. (Smart Insights)

Laptops and Desktops

But the rise of mobile browsing still doesn't overshadow the desktop. Every website should account equally for large desktop displays as well as the smaller screens of smartphones and tablets.
